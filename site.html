Perfect Blue: A Perfect Movie

Landing Page

This site is an analysis on one of my favorite movies that has changed how I view fame and pop culture. Although, it is not the most modern movie, it's themes are timeless and deeply disturbing. I broke up my analysis of Perfect Blue into three parts. Firstly, you will be introduced to the movie, then there's an exploration of each of the main characters, and lastly my final take on the movie and how it still stands the test of time.
